We are a Robotics and Artificial Intelligence lab in [the Department of Computer Engineering at Bilkent University](https://w3.cs.bilkent.edu.tr/en/). We work on intelligent robot assistants. Our current focus is on robotic manipulation. We merge the advances in deep learning with robotics to explore ideas like intuitive physics, task and motion planning, and ... .
